Common Tax Questions

The IRS provides a "Check your Refund" feature on their website. As well as additional helpful information regarding your refund status (See the link below) You can check the status of your 2022 income tax refund 24 hours after e-filing. Please allow 3 or 4 days after e-filing your 2020 and 2021 tax year returns. If you filed a paper return, please allow 4 weeks before checking your status. Check your Refund here at https://www.irs.gov/refunds

If you want to file your taxes for free. IRS Free File lets you prepare and file your federal income tax online using guided tax preparation, at an IRS partner site or Free File Fillable Forms. It's safe, easy and no cost to you for a federal return. What is IRS Free File? IRS Free File Program is a public-private partnership between the IRS and many tax preparation and filing software industry companies who provide their online tax preparation and filing for free. It provides two ways for taxpayers to prepare and file their federal income tax online for free:
  • Guided Tax Preparation provides free online tax preparation and filing at an IRS partner site. Our partners deliver this service at no cost to qualifying taxpayers. Taxpayers whose AGI is $73,000 or less qualify for a free federal tax return.
  • Free File Fillable Forms are electronic federal tax forms, equivalent to a paper 1040 form. You should know how to prepare your own tax return using form instructions and IRS publications if needed. It provides a free option to taxpayers whose income (AGI) is greater than $73,000.
    • Find what you need to get started, your protections and security, available forms and more about IRS Free File below.
For more information visit https://www.irs.gov/filing/free-file-do-your-federal-taxes-for-free#what

The IRS accepts full and partial payments, including payments toward a payment plan (including installment agreement). Penalties and interest will continue to grow until you pay the full balance. You will need to confirm your identity before making a payment using the pay now options. You can make a payment today, or schedule a payment, without signing up for an IRS Online Account. You can also pay from your bank account, your Debit or Credit Card, or even with digital wallet.

For more information visit https://www.irs.gov/payments

For any additional questions head to this IRS webpage https://www.irs.gov/help/ita

Credits & Deductions

Tax credits and deductions can change the amount of tax you owe so you pay less.
  • Credits can reduce the amount of tax you owe.
  • Deductions can reduce the amount of your income before you calculate the tax you owe.

Tax credits and deductions can change the amount of tax you owe so you pay less. Credits can reduce the amount of tax you owe. Deductions can reduce the amount of your income before you calculate the tax you owe.

When you claim certain credits and deductions on your tax return you may be able to get a larger refund, while others may give you a refund even if you don't owe any tax.

For more information regarding Credits and Deductions visit https://www.irs.gov/credits-and-deductions
